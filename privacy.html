Privacy Policy

Kitsinian Law Firm, APC · Last updated June 4, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Kitsinian Law Firm, APC ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information you provide through our websites, contact forms, and text messaging services.

Information We Collect

When you submit a contact form or otherwise communicate with us, we may collect: name, phone number, email address, mailing address, dates and details relating to your potential claim, and any other information you voluntarily provide. When you opt in to SMS communications, we also collect your mobile phone number and your consent record.

How We Use Information

We use the information you provide to evaluate your potential claim, communicate with you about your inquiry, schedule appointments, send appointment reminders, send secure links for e-signing documents, and otherwise provide legal services. We use SMS communications only for customer care purposes related to your matter.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell your information. We may share your information with: our attorneys and staff working on your matter; subcontractors and service providers that support our operations (for example, electronic signature platforms, customer relationship management tools, secure document storage, and case management software) strictly under confidentiality obligations; co-counsel or referred counsel when you have consented to such referral; and where required by law, court order, or to respond to lawful legal process.

No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. All the above categories exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with any third parties, excluding aggregators and providers of the text message services.

Your Choices

You may opt out of SMS communications at any time by replying "STOP" to any message you receive from us. You may also contact us to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information, subject to applicable legal and ethical retention requirements for client records.

Data Security

We maintain reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect the information we collect. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, however, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.
